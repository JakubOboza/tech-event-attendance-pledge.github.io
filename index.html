This site is a barebones basic way to track people pledging not to attend events where a specific person is present. Please file pull requests or issues to add your voice.

This site exists to collect information about people who wish to avoid attending events where a specific person is in attendance. It makes no claims about the intent of the people signing the list beyond their wish to publicly state they will avoid that person. The list also does not make any claims about the reasons for why a person should be avoided. It may link to reference materials from others, when relevant.

One of the hopes for creating this site is that it will be a useful resource for event organizers when they are organizing their events.
