--- layout: default title: Tech Event Attendance Pledge ---

This site is a barebones basic way to track people pledging not to attend events for specific reasons that are important to them. Please file pull requests or issues to add your voice.

This site may provide information about people who wish to avoid attending events where a specific person is in attendance. It makes no claims about the intent of the people signing the list beyond their wish to publicly state they will avoid that person. The list also does not make any claims about the reasons for why a person should be avoided. It may link to reference materials from others, when relevant.

One of the hopes for creating this site is that it will be a useful resource for event organizers when they are organizing their events.

This site is not intended to address all issues that may be directly or tangentially related to these pledges. It is attempting to make sure that our events continue to be safe spaces. This is one small part of what should be larger processes.

This site is readily open to pull requests and change. These lists should not be seen as set in stone, but a work in progress as part of what should be larger accountability processes.
